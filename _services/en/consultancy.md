We work with people who have to answer for the next tech decision at their company. CTOs who need to validate architecture before committing a year of roadmap. Product leads with a release on the line. Founders looking for an independent second opinion before signing.

Our method literally puts the client at the center. Each week, a working session with you and whoever you decide to include. Each session, a concrete outcome you take home in writing and a clear decision you can bring back to your committee with confidence.

## When it makes sense

- You are about to launch and want to validate technical decisions before committing budget.
- Your team is dragging technical debt and needs a thread to pull on.
- You are close to signing a large tech investment and want a second opinion aligned only with your interests.
- You have a product that works, but every new feature costs more than the last and you have to justify it.

## How we work

Short, frequent sessions. Actionable deliverables every week. A final plan your team can execute on their own if tomorrow we stop working together.

That is what good consulting looks like: leave the client in a better place than they were and leave the decision backed by judgment.
